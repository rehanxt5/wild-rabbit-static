A Taste of
Pure Comfort

Welcome to Wild Rabbit Cafe and Bakehouse, where every cup tells a story and every bite feels like home. Located in the heart of Bangalore, we are a sanctuary for coffee lovers and food enthusiasts alike.

Our philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients, craft everything with love, and serve it with a smile. Whether you're here for our signature sourdough toast, a quiet corner to read, or a lively brunch with friends, we promise an experience that warms your soul.
